How to Become a Member

If you would like to become a member, please send us an email at 5grams.aws@gmail.com. To become a member, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Requirement 1: Be an active member of AWS.
    • AWS Website
  • Requirement 2: Send and email to 5grams.aws@gmail.com with evidence.
  • Requirement 3: In the email also add your basic information: Name, phone number, email, and reason for joining.
